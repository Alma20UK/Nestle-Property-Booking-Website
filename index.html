Welcome to Duke Street

A Georgian apartment located in the centre of Bath

Welcome to Duke Street, Bath. A gorgeous two bed apartment in the center of the city of Bath. The apartment is also within a 5 minute walk of the Roman Baths and the Bath Abbey. Very much in the heart of the city and within a stones throw of many of Bath's restaurants and pubs. It's the perfect place for your stay in the city.

The Space

The apartment has two large bedrooms. One large double and a second with two single beds. The king sized bed can be split into singles so it's possible to have four separate beds in total if you wish. The larger of the two rooms is lovely and bright during the day with nice views of the surrounding Georgian architecture that can be seen through the traditional sash windows.

The kitchen is very functional and has an oven/ hob and plenty of utensils, pots and pans should you require them.

The apartment has a luxurious bathroom and is supplied with soap, shampoo and shower cream. Perfect for a relaxing bath after a day exploration round the city.
